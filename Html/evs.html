// 02 — THE SOLUTION

Rethinking The System.

Modern RWH is not just a barrel under a pipe. It is a complete engineered system that captures, filters, stores and reuses water right where it falls.

ANIMATION · COMPLETE RWH WATER FLOW — ROOF → FILTER → STORAGE TANK → DISTRIBUTION
// 03 — HOW IT WORKS

The 4 Critical Parts.

Every modern RWH system is built on four key pillars — each one connected seamlessly to the next.

01
🏠
Catchment Area
Rooftop, terrace or paved surface — designed with the right slope and smooth material to collect the maximum amount of rainfall with zero loss.
02
🌀
Conveyance
Sealed gutters and integrated pipes carry rainwater directly to the filter unit — no contamination, no leaks, no evaporation loss.
03
⚗️
Filtration
Multi-stage vortex self-cleaning filters remove leaves, sand and fine particles automatically. Zero manual cleaning needed, ever.
04
🗄️
Storage & Use
Smart underground modular tanks store clean water. IoT-connected pumps supply it on demand — toilets, gardens, cooling systems and more.
ANIMATION · CROSS-SECTION SHOWING ALL 4 COMPONENTS WORKING TOGETHER
// 04 — SMART TECHNOLOGY

Intelligence in Harvesting.

This is the real game-changer. Smart RWH systems do not just collect water — they think, predict and automatically adapt.

📡
IoT Sensors
Monitor tank level, pH, turbidity and flow rate in real-time, 24 hours a day — no manual checks needed.
🧠
Predictive AI
Reads 7-day weather forecast. Before heavy rain, it pre-releases stored water to make room for fresh capture — maximizing efficiency.
📱
Smartphone App
See tank level, water quality score and monthly savings live from your phone. Instant alert if there is a leak or fault.
☁️
Cloud Reports
Full historical data, usage trends and automated maintenance reminders — all stored securely in the cloud.

// 05 — URBAN SOLUTIONS

RWH for Concrete Jungles.

Even in the most densely built cities, modern RWH solutions fit in invisibly — under pavements, parking lots and roads.

ANIMATION · SIDE-BY-SIDE COMPARISON — STANDARD vs PERMEABLE SURFACE WATER BEHAVIOUR
❌ Standard Parking Lot
  • Surface heats up to 60°C+
  • 95% of rain becomes runoff
  • Zero water absorption
  • Causes downstream flooding
  • Groundwater never recharged
✅ Permeable Lot + RWH
  • Absorbs 80–100% of rainfall
  • Cools surface by 15–20°C
  • Recharges groundwater tables
  • Stores water for daily reuse
  • Prevents downstream flooding
